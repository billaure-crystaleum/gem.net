GEM aka CRFI, CRYSTAL and various symbols of xAssets family of community operated educational projects is the first public and private dual parallel chain offering interoperability like no other, comining strengths of public and privacy centric blockchains, the best of both worlds with sidechains offering proof-of-work and proof-of-authority! If you are familiar with Monero, and Ethereum you understand Geth, Nethermind, Hyperledger Besu (formerly Pantheon), and EthereumJS, Web3, that all works the same for Crystaleum. So you will be right at home with the community. If you need help just reach out on Telegram at Crystaleum. This aka CRFI is a community-based project, completely open-source, naturally. It was born in September 2020 with a friendship and a handshake, the first year the market suffered an attack on Proof-of-Work and so we forked Ethereum and from there the project has been reorganizing, heavily involved in ongoing research and development of new and existing tokens, coins, protocols and technologies, and growing in contributors ever since.
